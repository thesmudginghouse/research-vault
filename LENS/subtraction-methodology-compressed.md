## CORE PRINCIPLE

> "Most UFOs are not UFOs. This is not a dismissal. It is the prerequisite for finding the ones that are."

The Carter Subtraction Methodology is a **signal isolation framework**.
It applies identically across all domains — UAP, water science, plasma physics, genomics, legal analysis, engineering.

**The invariant logic:**
1. Cast the widest possible net (collect all observations/data/reports)
2. Subtract what is explainable by known categories — rigorously, in order
3. The residual that survives all subtractions is the signal
4. The signal is real. Protect it. Investigate it.

## THE FOUR-CATEGORY TAXONOMY

Applied sequentially. Never skip to Category 4.

| Category | Label | Description | Typical proportion |
|----------|-------|-------------|-------------------|
| **1** | Prosaic | Known natural phenomena, publicly known technology, misidentification due to human perceptual limits under stress | 60–65% of reports |
| **2** | Classified / Hidden | Human technology or activity beyond public knowledge — black programs, undisclosed operations, suppressed data | Large but unknown |
| **3** | Industry Artifact | Cases inflated by economic, ideological, or institutional incentive — mythology, hypnotic regression, delayed recollection contaminated by narrative | Variable |
| **4** | Genuine Residual | What remains after rigorous elimination of 1, 2, 3. Small in number. Extraordinary in significance. | Dozens, not thousands |

**The key insight:** Category 4 is HIDDEN by the volume of Categories 1, 2, and 3. The noise is the strategy — whether designed or emergent, the effect is identical. The genuine signal is statistically invisible in an unsubtracted dataset.

## THE SEVEN-STEP PROTOCOL

### Step 1 — Strip the retelling
Find original documentation closest to the event. Official report before explanation was applied. Witness statement before narrative contamination. Physical measurement before interpretation. The retelling is not the evidence.

### Step 2 — Apply taxonomy before reading accounts
Run Category 1 → 2 → 3 in order. Do not read the accounts first — they will anchor you to the interpretation. Apply the taxonomy cold.

### Step 3 — Weight physical evidence above testimonial
Human memory is reconstructive. Testimony under stress is unreliable. Testimony collected years later in a narrative-saturated environment is nearly uninterpretable. Physical effects (radiation, EM interference, biological symptoms, sensor data, trace evidence) are not immune to error but are not subject to suggestion.

### Step 4 — Ask who benefits from each explanation
Prosaic explanation may protect a classified program. Spectacular explanation may sustain a commercial ecosystem. Neither motivation makes the explanation wrong. But understanding the motivation reveals what is missing.

### Step 5 — Separate craft from context
Location near infrastructure is evidence of proximity to operations, not evidence of alien interest. Apply geographic and operational context before assigning to Category 4.

### Step 6 — Respect the residual
Do not inflate it into grand theory. Do not deflate it into dismissal. The residual is real. It is small. It is the most significant thing in the dataset. Treat it accordingly.

### Step 7 — Distrust certainty
Anyone claiming to know the complete answer is selling something. The honest position is: taxonomy of mostly-explained, some-classified, a-few-genuinely-unknown. The unknown is where the work lives.

## EVIDENCE HIERARCHY (highest to lowest reliability)

1. Contemporaneous physical measurements (radiation, EM, trace, sensor telemetry)
2. Original documentation closest to event (filed before explanation applied)
3. Multi-system sensor confirmation (independent sensors, same contact)
4. Testimony from trained observers with no ideological investment
5. Testimony from credentialed insiders (filtered through their classification level)
6. Civilian witness accounts (raw, pre-narrative)
7. Hypnotic regression / delayed recollection (uninterpretable as primary evidence)
8. Industry-processed accounts (treat as Category 3 unless original sources available)

## THE INDUSTRY STRUCTURE AWARENESS MODULE

Before ingesting any secondary source in any domain, evaluate:
- **Revenue dependency:** Does the source's income depend on a particular conclusion?
- **Exclusion history:** Has the source excluded competing analyses rather than engaging them?
- **Retelling distance:** How many layers of interpretation separate the source from primary data?
- **Consistency incentive:** Does the source's institutional position require a stable narrative?

Funding relationships do not require instruction to produce bias. Incentive alignment is sufficient.
